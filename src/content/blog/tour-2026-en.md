## SABBAT'S BACK! — Seven Deadly Sins

The Sabbat has returned, wielding the power of its most unholy sacraments.

The Italian Tour 2026 spans **7 stages**, each dedicated to one of the **Seven Deadly Sins**.
Every event is a chance to compete, collect, and leave your mark on the season.

At each stage, participants can earn a **unique mark**, tied to the sin and event location.
These marks are not just symbolic: accumulated throughout the Tour, they grant access to a **dedicated secondary ranking**, also packed with prizes.

Not only the strongest or luckiest players will be rewarded, but also the most **consistent and present**.
Because in the Italian Tour 2026, even when results don't meet expectations, the Sabbat watches... and rewards.
