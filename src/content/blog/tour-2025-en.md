**A year of challenges across Italy**

Blood flows from north to south: the Italian Tour 2025 brings **Vampire: The Eternal Struggle** to major Italian cities with a series of independent stages, each a standalone tournament, but all linked by an overall ranking. The best players in the country will compete for precious points in every city, until the absolute Tour 2025 champions are crowned.
