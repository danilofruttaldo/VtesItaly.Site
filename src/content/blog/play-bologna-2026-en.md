On **May 22, 23 and 24, 2026** the Italian community of _Vampire: The Eternal Struggle_ will be at **Play – Festival del Gioco** at **BolognaFiere** with **open demo tables** for anyone who wants to step into the longest-running political card game in the hobby.

Three days, one invitation: **come and try VTES**. We'll be in **Hall 18**, in the **Vampire: The Masquerade area** (booth **J11 — Camarilla Italia** / **J13 — Area Vampiri**). Whether you've never seen the game or you've only heard about it, you'll find a table ready, the decks already set up, and players from the Italian community to walk you through a 30–45 minute taster round. No registration, no deck to bring — just sit down.

The booth will be staffed in shifts by **players from different cities** — a chance to meet who plays near you and find out where the game runs in your area.

## Where to find us — Hall 18

**VTES** is the meaner cousin of **Vampire: The Masquerade**: same factions, same World of Darkness, same political intrigue — but on the card table, with five vampires bleeding, voting, blocking and fighting each other. If you spot the VTM community booths, you're already there.

> [!NOTE]
> **Play** is Italy's largest festival dedicated to board, role-playing and card games: three days at BolognaFiere with over 200 exhibitors, 3,000 free-play tables and 700 events. On Saturday the 23rd the fair stays open until 23:00. For tickets, map and full programme visit [play-festival.it](https://www.play-festival.it/).

See you at the table.
